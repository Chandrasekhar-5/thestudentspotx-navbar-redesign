Campus Team Recruitment

Become a campus representative and help grow The Student Spot community in your college.

Your Role

Act as a campus representative, promote events, and help connect students with opportunities.

What You Gain

Leadership experience, certificates, recognition, and exposure to industry networks.

Make an Impact

Help fellow students discover opportunities and build a vibrant innovation community on your campus.

Who Can Apply?

We're looking for enthusiastic students who want to make a difference on their campus.

1

Active College Students

Currently enrolled in undergraduate or postgraduate programs with at least one year remaining.

2

Good Communication Skills

Ability to communicate effectively with students, faculty, and our team to promote campus activities.

3

Leadership Interest

Demonstrated interest in leadership, community building, and organizing campus events.

Leadership Experience

Certificates & Recognition

Networking Opportunities
